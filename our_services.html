Extra Information:

  • When booking a venue please ensure that you as the client have arranged with the owners of the venue that you are allowed to use their venue to be photographed.
    This must be done before hand or before comfirming where you will need the photographer to meet you at the location that as been given to him/her for the photoshoot.

  • Please note extra costs will be charged when the venue for the photoshoot is more than 30km away from the office's venues.
    It will be added to ones cost when the location has been sent through to our organisation. It will be added to the quote as one will receive to options to choose from, which states the cost of both options in case one decides to change the location of the photoshoot.

  • Photoshoots that have less than 100 photos will be either emailed or a link will be sent out to the customer depending on the request that was made on how to recieve their photos.
    Photoshoots that have more than 100 photos will be sent a link that they can use to access the photos, as one cannot recieve it through email as it the files will be too large to send through email.
